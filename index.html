About

Welcome to the Catholic AI Project. This initiative is dedicated to exploring what the Vatican has said about artificial intelligence and examining its implications. My goal is to provide clear insights and resources to help people understand and engage with the evolving conversation around AI from a Catholic perspective.

The information for this website comes mostly from a search for "artificial intelligence" (exact match) in the website of the Vatican. I retrieved and reviewed all 97 documents returned by the search as of April the 27th, 2026.
